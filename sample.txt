Carlos Alcaraz reached the US Open semifinals with a blend of aggressive returning and authoritative groundstrokes.
He showcased exceptional movement, converting key break chances in tight sets and finishing rallies at the net when opportunities arose.
His serve was a critical weapon in pressure moments, helping him secure two decisive sets in the middle of the match while battle-tested from a taxing, high-stakes atmosphere. 
The run underscored his versatility on hard courts and reinforced his status as a top contender for majors.
